Visit Us

Our lectures and events are held at the historic Saroyan Theatre in downtown Fresno. We encourage you to attend, meet fellow enthusiasts, and share in the excitement of paleontology. Directions and parking information are available on our Tickets page.

Get Involved

We invite you to become part of our community. Volunteer opportunities include assisting at events, helping with educational outreach, and supporting field trips. Membership packages are available for individuals, families, and patrons who wish to support our mission of bringing paleontology to the San Joaquin Valley.

We look forward to hearing from you and welcoming you into our community of fossil enthusiasts and lifelong learners.
